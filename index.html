Projects

Restate is a comprehensive app designed for the construction industry, combining e-commerce and rental services. It enables users to buy, sell, and rent construction equipment, materials, and tools seamlessly. With an intuitive interface and robust functionality, Restate simplifies operations, enhances accessibility, and streamlines workflows for construction professionals.

BiteBox is a food delivery app tailored for college canteens, designed to simplify meal ordering for students. It allows users to browse menus, place orders, and get food delivered directly to their block. With a user-friendly interface and efficient delivery system, BiteBox enhances convenience and ensures students can enjoy their meals without long waits.

HappySchool is an innovative platform designed for teachers to provide courses that focus on student discipline and personal growth. The app features a dynamic feed, engaging courses, challenges, and interactive help sessions to support both teaching and learning. With a structured approach and user-friendly design, HappySchool empowers educators to guide students toward academic success and character development.
